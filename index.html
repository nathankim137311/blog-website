About Me

9.2.2021

I am a aspiring full-stack web-developer from Seattle. I am creating this website from scratch, going through the entire production pipeline: from front-end to back-end, as well as hosting and authentication. I am doing all of this in order to cement all the concepts I am learning and applying it to my own personal projects, this blog being one of them. The website probably doesn't look any good... but it will always be a work in progress.
